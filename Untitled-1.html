Acrylic on Canvas | 52 x 50 inches

Berenice

Price: N5,600,000

Berenice is another masterpiece spurred out of a major shift in the artist's life. Berenice (Ancient Greek) means 'the bearer of Victory'.

The painting references the concept of a battleground, igniting forces of pain, and darkness paradoxically leading to the beam of light at the end of the tunnel, thus sprinkling hope needed to again re-live strength and victory, momentarily lost in the raging whirlpool of life.
