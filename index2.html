Berenice

Price:N5,600,000

Berenice is another masterpiece spurred out of a major shift in the artist's life. Berenice (Ancient Greek) means 'the bearer of Victory'.

The painting references the concept of a battleground. Igniting forces of pain, darkness paradoxically leading to the beam of light at the end of the tunnel, thus sprinkling hope needed to again re-live strength and victory. Momentarily lost in the raging whirlpool of life
